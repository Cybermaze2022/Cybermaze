About Us

“Predicting the Future isn’t Magic. It’s Artificial Intelligence”

Blue Bells Model School believes in a world where all children are empowered to take their learning into their own hands, a world where their passion, curiosity and creativity are ignited. With this objective, the school under the able guidance and patronage of Ms. Anshuka Aneja, Deputy Director IT, Blue Bells Group of Schools is organizing Cyber Maze 2022, a Mega Intra School IT Fest. The fest is hosted to bring forth the dynamism and prolific aspect of technology in the hands of tech-savvy generation.

The theme for this year is AIoT. A plethora of activities are organized for classes III to XII. The creativity and skills of the participants will be adjudged by eminent Faculty members.

Ms. Anshuka Aneja

Deputy Director IT

Ms. Anshuka Aneja, Deputy Director IT, Blue Bells Group of Schools believes that the right blend of Technology in Education can do wonders for teaching and learning in schools.
